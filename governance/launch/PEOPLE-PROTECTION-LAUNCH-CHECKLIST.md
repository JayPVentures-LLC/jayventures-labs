# People Protection Launch Checklist

Version: 1.0  
Status: Active

Use this checklist before production launch, public publication, payment activation, partner onboarding, AI-assisted decision workflow activation, or access-routing deployment.

## Required

- [ ] The change has a clear purpose.
- [ ] The affected users, creators, partners, or admins are identified.
- [ ] The People Protection Enforcement Matrix has been reviewed.
- [ ] Public-facing copy is clear and not misleading.
- [ ] Pricing, perks, access, cancellation, and refund details are clear where applicable.
- [ ] No hidden monetization is introduced.
- [ ] No exploitative engagement loop is introduced.
- [ ] No discriminatory targeting is introduced.
- [ ] No social scoring is introduced.
- [ ] No unlawful surveillance is introduced.
- [ ] No biometric mass tracking is introduced.
- [ ] AI-assisted decisions include human review where people are materially affected.
- [ ] Appeal or escalation path exists where access, eligibility, membership, or participation can be denied or restricted.
- [ ] Vendor dependencies are mapped.
- [ ] Data collection is limited to operational necessity.
- [ ] Rollback or remediation path exists.
- [ ] Evidence, policy, or audit artifacts are versioned.
- [ ] Reviewer and date are recorded.
