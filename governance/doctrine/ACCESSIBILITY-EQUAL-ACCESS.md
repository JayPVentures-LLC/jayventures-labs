# Accessibility and Equal Access Standard

Version: 1.0  
Status: Active

## Purpose

This standard ensures that public access points, support routes, terms, appeals, payment explanations, and trust documents remain understandable and usable.

## Core Rule

Core public access points, terms, appeals, support routes, and payment explanations should be understandable, accessible, and usable across common devices and assistive needs.

## Requirements

Where feasible, public-facing systems should support:

- Plain-language explanation.
- Mobile usability.
- Keyboard navigation.
- Readable contrast.
- Clear headings.
- Accessible forms.
- Error messages that explain how to recover.
- Support routes for access problems.
- Appeal routes that do not require unnecessary technical knowledge.

## Anti-Exclusion

Access systems may not intentionally exclude people through confusing language, inaccessible interfaces, hidden pathways, or unnecessary friction.
