# Brand and Representation Integrity Standard

Version: 1.0  
Status: Active

## Purpose

This standard prevents public confusion across JayPVentures LLC, jaypventures, jaypVLabs, JPV-OS, JPV Institute, products, partners, and access pathways.

## Core Rule

Public materials must clearly distinguish enterprise infrastructure, creator ecosystem, labs/research, institutional doctrine, and access products. No page, partner statement, campaign, badge, credential, or claim may imply authority, certification, partnership, endorsement, employment, legal status, institutional status, or public authority that has not been verified.

## Required Distinctions

Public materials should distinguish:

- JayPVentures LLC: enterprise infrastructure and operational execution.
- jaypventures: creator ecosystem and venture studio.
- jaypVLabs: research, validation, and controlled innovation.
- JPV-OS: governance, routing, entitlement, and infrastructure doctrine.
- JPV Institute: standards, doctrine, research, accreditation concepts, and infrastructure literacy.

## Partner Recognition

Partner or vendor recognition must not imply formal partnership, endorsement, sponsorship, certification, or authorization unless such relationship has been verified.

## Prohibited Claims

Public materials may not claim:

- False institutional authority.
- Unverified government affiliation.
- Unverified certification power.
- Unverified partner endorsement.
- Misleading legal authority.
- Misleading educational accreditation.
- Misleading platform authorization.
