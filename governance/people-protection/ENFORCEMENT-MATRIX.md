# People Protection Enforcement Matrix

Version: 1.0  
Status: Active  
Applies to: JPV-OS, JayPVentures LLC, jaypventures, jaypVLabs, JPV Institute, Access Gateway, partner workflows, automation, AI behavior, entitlement routing, membership systems, and public-facing infrastructure.

## Purpose

This matrix converts People Protection principles into operational enforcement. Every major product, workflow, integration, partner pathway, entitlement decision, and deployment must be traceable to a protection principle, an enforcement point, required evidence, and a failure action.

The standard is simple:

If a principle is required, the system must show where it is enforced.

## Required Launch Evidence

Every production launch should include:

1. People Protection matrix review.
2. Public trust copy review where public users are affected.
3. Partner or vendor review when third-party systems are involved.
4. Appeal path when a person can be denied, restricted, removed, downgraded, or materially affected.
5. Rollback or remediation path.
6. Audit trail with commit hash, reviewer, date, and decision reason.

## Non-Negotiable Rule

No product, workflow, integration, or automation may remove human dignity, appealability, transparency, reversibility, interoperability freedom, or anti-exploitation safeguards for convenience, speed, monetization, partnership pressure, or platform dependency.
