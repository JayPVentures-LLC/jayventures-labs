# Decision Log

Status: v0.1 operating draft

## Purpose

The decision log records accepted, rejected, deferred, and superseded research decisions.

Every major framework change should point back to a decision entry.

## Decision format

| ID | Date | Decision | Framework | Status | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| RP-DEC-0001 | 2026-07-02 | Place ASO and IAT in Labs as experimental research artifacts rather than production governance doctrine. | Research Platform | Accepted | Prevents premature public or production claims. |
| RP-DEC-0002 | 2026-07-02 | Use proposal-first workflow for new concepts. | Research Platform | Accepted | Prevents silent foundation drift. |

## Status values

- Proposed
- Accepted
- Accepted for Draft
- Rejected
- Deferred
- Superseded
- Retired

## Required decision details

Future decisions should include:

- problem;
- alternatives considered;
- decision;
- affected files;
- rationale;
- risks;
- follow-up review date if needed.
