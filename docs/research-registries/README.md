# Research Registries

Status: v0.1 operating draft  
Lane: jaypVLabs research, validation, and experimentation  
Owner: JayPVentures LLC

## Purpose

Research registries provide stable identifiers and audit trails for concepts, terms, theorems, hypotheses, proposals, decisions, measurements, and domain mappings.

They prevent foundational drift by making every accepted, rejected, revised, or pending research item visible.

## Registries

- `TERM_REGISTRY.md` — canonical term identifiers and definitions.
- `THEOREM_REGISTRY.md` — theorem candidates and proof status.
- `HYPOTHESIS_REGISTRY.md` — empirical hypotheses and test status.
- `DECISION_LOG.md` — accepted, rejected, deferred, or superseded research decisions.
- `MEASUREMENT_REGISTRY.md` — constructs requiring operational definitions and validation.
- `DOMAIN_MAP_REGISTRY.md` — cross-domain translation targets.

## Identifier convention

Use stable IDs.

Examples:

- `ASO-PRIM-001`
- `ASO-FUNC-101`
- `IAT-STATE-001`
- `IAT-TRANS-001`
- `RP-DEC-0001`
- `ASO-HYP-0001`

Identifiers are never reused after retirement.

## Review rule

A concept can be discussed without an identifier, but it cannot become part of a specification, measurement system, theorem registry, or dictionary until assigned one.
