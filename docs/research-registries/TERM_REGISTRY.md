# Term Registry

Status: v0.1 operating draft

## Purpose

The term registry assigns stable identifiers to canonical research terms. Definitions may evolve through versioned review, but identifiers must not be reused.

## Change rule

Any term revision must include:

- current definition;
- proposed definition;
- reason for change;
- affected artifacts;
- backward compatibility note;
- decision record.
