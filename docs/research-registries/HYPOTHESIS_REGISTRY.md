# Hypothesis Registry

Status: v0.1 operating draft

## Purpose

The hypothesis registry tracks empirical claims requiring observation, measurement, simulation, or external validation.

Hypotheses are allowed to fail. Failed hypotheses remain part of the record.

## Required hypothesis record

Each hypothesis must eventually include:

- statement;
- framework;
- variables;
- measurement protocol;
- predicted outcome;
- disconfirmation condition;
- data source;
- status;
- result;
- revision notes.
