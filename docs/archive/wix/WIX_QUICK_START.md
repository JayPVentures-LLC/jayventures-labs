## 5. QUICK STYLING NOTES

- **Headings:** Bold, confident, 40-50px for H1
- **Body Copy:** 16-18px, 1.6 line height, dark gray (not pure black)
- **Buttons:** Solid background, white text, 14-16px
- **Sections:** 60-80px padding top/bottom, keep breathing room
- **Color Scheme:** Use your primary brand color for buttons/accents
- **Mobile:** Test each section; ensure text doesn't cramp
