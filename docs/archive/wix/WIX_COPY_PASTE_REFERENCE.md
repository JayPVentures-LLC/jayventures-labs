## 🎨 STYLING QUICK NOTES

**Headings:** Bold, confident. H1 larger (40-50px). Subheadings 24-32px.

**Body Copy:** 16-18px, line-height 1.6, dark gray (not black).

**Buttons:** Your brand color, white text, 14-16px, rounded corners.

**Spacing:** 60-80px top/bottom padding on sections. Let content breathe.

**Mobile:** Stack sections vertically, ensure text doesn't cramp, buttons remain tappable.
